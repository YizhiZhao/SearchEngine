MONTANO :  Pray heavens he be; For I have served him, and the man commands Like a full soldier. Let's to the seaside, ho! As well to see the vessel that's come in As to throw out our eyes for brave Othello, Even till we make the main and the aerial blue An indistinct regard.  Third Gentleman  Come, let's do so: For every minute is expectancy Of more arrivance. Enter CASSIO
