DUKE OF VENICE :  Nay, in all confidence, he's not for Rhodes.  First Officer  Here is more news. Enter a Messenger  Messenger  The Ottomites, reverend and gracious, Steering with due course towards the isle of Rhodes, Have there injointed them with an after fleet.  First Senator  Ay, so I thought. How many, as you guess?  Messenger  Of thirty sail: and now they do restem Their backward course, bearing with frank appearance Their purposes toward Cyprus. Signior Montano, Your trusty and most valiant servitor, With his free duty recommends you thus, And prays you to believe him.
