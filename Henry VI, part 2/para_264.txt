KING HENRY VI :  Go, take hence that traitor from our sight; For his death we do perceive his guilt: And God in justice hath revealed to us The truth and innocence of this poor fellow, Which he had thought to have murder'd wrongfully. Come, fellow, follow us for thy reward. Sound a flourish. Exeunt  SCENE IV. A street.  Enter GLOUCESTER and his Servingmen, in mourning cloaks
