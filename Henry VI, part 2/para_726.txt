YOUNG CLIFFORD :  But that my heart's on future mischief set, I would speak blasphemy ere bid you fly: But fly you must; uncurable discomfit Reigns in the hearts of all our present parts. Away, for your relief! and we will live To see their day and them our fortune give: Away, my lord, away! Exeunt  SCENE III. Fields near St. Alban's.  Alarum. Retreat.
