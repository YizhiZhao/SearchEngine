CORNWALL :  Edmund, farewell. Exeunt GONERIL, EDMUND, and OSWALD Go seek the traitor Gloucester, Pinion him like a thief, bring him before us. Exeunt other Servants Though well we may not pass upon his life Without the form of justice, yet our power Shall do a courtesy to our wrath, which men May blame, but not control.
