KENT :  Well, sir, I'll bring you to our master Lear, And leave you to attend him: some dear cause Will in concealment wrap me up awhile; When I am known aright, you shall not grieve Lending me this acquaintance. I pray you, go Along with me. Exeunt  SCENE IV. The same. A tent.  Enter, with drum and colours, CORDELIA, Doctor, and Soldiers
