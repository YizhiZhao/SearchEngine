GONERIL :  Pray you, content. What, Oswald, ho! To the Fool You, sir, more knave than fool, after your master.  Fool  Nuncle Lear, nuncle Lear, tarry and take the fool with thee. A fox, when one has caught her, And such a daughter, Should sure to the slaughter, If my cap would buy a halter: So the fool follows after. Exit
