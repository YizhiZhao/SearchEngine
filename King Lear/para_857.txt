ALBANY :  She is not well; convey her to  my tent. Exit Regan, led Enter a Herald Come hither, herald,--Let the trumpet sound, And read out this.  Captain  Sound, trumpet! A trumpet sounds  Herald  [Reads]  'If any man of quality or degree within the lists of the army will maintain upon Edmund, supposed Earl of Gloucester, that he is a manifold traitor, let him appear by the third sound of the trumpet: he is bold in his defence.'
