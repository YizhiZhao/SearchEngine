CLOTEN :  I would this music would come: I am advised to give her music o' mornings; they say it will penetrate. Enter Musicians Come on; tune: if you can penetrate her with your fingering, so; we'll try with tongue too: if none will do, let her remain; but I'll never give o'er. First, a very excellent good-conceited thing; after, a wonderful sweet air, with admirable rich words to it: and then let her consider.
