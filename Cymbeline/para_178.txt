CLOTEN :  Was there ever man had such luck! when I kissed the jack, upon an up-cast to be hit away! I had a hundred pound on't: and then a whoreson jackanapes must take me up for swearing; as if I borrowed mine oaths of him and might not spend them at my pleasure.  First Lord  What got he by that? You have broke his pate with your bowl.  Second Lord  [Aside]  If his wit had been like him that broke it, it would have run all out.
