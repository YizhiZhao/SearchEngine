IMOGEN :  I see before me, man: nor here, nor here, Nor what ensues, but have a fog in them, That I cannot look through. Away, I prithee; Do as I bid thee: there's no more to say, Accessible is none but Milford way. Exeunt  SCENE III. Wales: a mountainous country with a cave.
