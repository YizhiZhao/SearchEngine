POSTHUMUS LEONATUS :  I tell thee, fellow, there are none want eyes to direct them the way I am going, but such as wink and will not use them.  First Gaoler  What an infinite mock is this, that a man should have the best use of eyes to see the way of blindness! I am sure hanging's the way of winking. Enter a Messenger  Messenger  Knock off his manacles; bring your prisoner to the king.
