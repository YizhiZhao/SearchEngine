IMOGEN :  About some half-hour hence, I pray you, speak with me: you shall at least Go see my lord aboard: for this time leave me. Exeunt  SCENE II. The same. A public place.  Enter CLOTEN and two Lords  First Lord  Sir, I would advise you to shift a shirt; the violence of action hath made you reek as a sacrifice: where air comes out, air comes in: there's none abroad so wholesome as that you vent.
