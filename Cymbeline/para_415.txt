PISANIO :  Thou bid'st me to my loss: for true to thee Were to prove false, which I will never be, To him that is most true. To Milford go, And find not her whom thou pursuest. Flow, flow, You heavenly blessings, on her! This fool's speed Be cross'd with slowness; labour be his meed! Exit  SCENE VI. Wales. Before the cave of Belarius.  Enter IMOGEN, in boy's clothes
