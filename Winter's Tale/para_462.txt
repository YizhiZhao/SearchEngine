AUTOLYCUS :  A lie; you are rough and hairy. Let me have no lying: it becomes none but tradesmen, and they often give us soldiers the lie: but we pay them for it with stamped coin, not stabbing steel; therefore they do not give us the lie.  Clown  Your worship had like to have given us one, if you had not taken yourself with the manner.  Shepherd  Are you a courtier, an't like you, sir?
