AUTOLYCUS :  How blessed are we that are not simple men! Yet nature might have made me as these are, Therefore I will not disdain.  Clown  This cannot be but a great courtier.  Shepherd  His garments are rich, but he wears them not handsomely.  Clown  He seems to be the more noble in being fantastical: a great man, I'll warrant; I know by the picking on's teeth.
