DROMIO OF SYRACUSE :  Nay, she is worse, she is the devil's dam; and here she comes in the habit of a light wench: and thereof comes that the wenches say 'God damn me;' that's as much to say 'God make me a light wench.' It is written, they appear to men like angels of light: light is an effect of fire, and fire will burn; ergo, light wenches will burn. Come not near her.  Courtezan  Your man and you are marvellous merry, sir. Will you go with me? We'll mend our dinner here?
