AEGEON :  Hopeless and helpless doth AEgeon wend, But to procrastinate his lifeless end. Exeunt  SCENE II. The Mart.  Enter ANTIPHOLUS of Syracuse, DROMIO of Syracuse, and First Merchant  First Merchant  Therefore give out you are of Epidamnum, Lest that your goods too soon be confiscate. This very day a Syracusian merchant Is apprehended for arrival here; And not being able to buy out his life According to the statute of the town, Dies ere the weary sun set in the west. There is your money that I had to keep.
