ADRIANA :  Peace, fool! thy master and his man are here, And that is false thou dost report to us.  Servant  Mistress, upon my life, I tell you true; I have not breathed almost since I did see it. He cries for you, and vows, if he can take you, To scorch your face and to disfigure you. Cry within Hark, hark! I hear him, mistress. fly, be gone!
