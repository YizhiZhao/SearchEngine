TRANIO :  'Tis death for any one in Mantua To come to Padua. Know you not the cause? Your ships are stay'd at Venice, and the duke, For private quarrel 'twixt your duke and him, Hath publish'd and proclaim'd it openly: 'Tis, marvel, but that you are but newly come, You might have heard it else proclaim'd about.  Pedant  Alas! sir, it is worse for me than so; For I have bills for money by exchange From Florence and must here deliver them.
