KING PHILIP :  Peace, lady! pause, or be more temperate: It ill beseems this presence to cry aim To these ill-tuned repetitions. Some trumpet summon hither to the walls These men of Angiers: let us hear them speak Whose title they admit, Arthur's or John's. Trumpet sounds. Enter certain Citizens upon the walls  First Citizen  Who is it that hath warn'd us to the walls?
