GLOUCESTER :  Faint-hearted Woodvile, prizest him 'fore me? Arrogant Winchester, that haughty prelate, Whom Henry, our late sovereign, ne'er could brook? Thou art no friend to God or to the king: Open the gates, or I'll shut thee out shortly. Serving-Men	Open the gates unto the lord protector, Or we'll burst them open, if that you come not quickly.
