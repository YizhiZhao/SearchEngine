TALBOT :  France, thou shalt rue this treason with thy tears, If Talbot but survive thy treachery. Pucelle, that witch, that damned sorceress, Hath wrought this hellish mischief unawares, That hardly we escaped the pride of France. Exit An alarum: excursions. BEDFORD, brought in sick  in a chair.
