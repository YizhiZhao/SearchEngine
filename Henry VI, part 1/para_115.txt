JOAN LA PUCELLE :  Talbot, farewell; thy hour is not yet come: I must go victual Orleans forthwith. A short alarum; then enter the town with soldiers O'ertake me, if thou canst; I scorn thy strength. Go, go, cheer up thy hungry-starved men; Help Salisbury to make his testament: This day is ours, as many more shall be. Exit
