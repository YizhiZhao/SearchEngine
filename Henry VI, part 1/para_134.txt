TALBOT :  And here will Talbot mount, or make his grave. Now, Salisbury, for thee, and for the right Of English Henry, shall this night appear How much in duty I am bound to both.  Sentinels  Arm! arm! the enemy doth make assault! Cry: 'St. George,' 'A Talbot.' The French leap over the walls in their shirts.  Enter, several ways, the BASTARD OF ORLEANS, ALENCON, and REIGNIER, half ready, and half unready
