MISTRESS QUICKLY :  Now heaven send thee good fortune! Exit FENTON A kind heart he hath: a woman would run through fire and water for such a kind heart. But yet I would my master had Mistress Anne; or I would Master Slender had her; or, in sooth, I would Master Fenton had her; I will do what I can for them all three; for so I have promised, and I'll be as good as my word; but speciously for Master Fenton. Well, I must of another errand to Sir John Falstaff from my two mistresses: what a beast am I to slack it! Exit  SCENE V. A room in the Garter Inn.  Enter FALSTAFF and BARDOLPH
