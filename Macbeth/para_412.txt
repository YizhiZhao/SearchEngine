LADY MACBETH :  Here's the smell of the blood still: all the perfumes of Arabia will not sweeten this little hand. Oh, oh, oh!  Doctor  What a sigh is there! The heart is sorely charged.  Gentlewoman  I would not have such a heart in my bosom for the dignity of the whole body.  Doctor  Well, well, well,--  Gentlewoman  Pray God it be, sir.  Doctor  This disease is beyond my practise: yet I have known those which have walked in their sleep who have died holily in their beds.
