BANQUO :  O, treachery! Fly, good Fleance, fly, fly, fly! Thou mayst revenge. O slave! Dies. FLEANCE escapes  Third Murderer  Who did strike out the light?  First Murderer  Wast not the way?  Third Murderer  There's but one down; the son is fled.  Second Murderer  We have lost Best half of our affair.  First Murderer  Well, let's away, and say how much is done. Exeunt  SCENE IV. The same.
