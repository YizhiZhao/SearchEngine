ACT IV : SCENE I. A cavern. In the middle, a boiling cauldron.  Thunder. Enter the three Witches  First Witch  Thrice the brinded cat hath mew'd.  Second Witch  Thrice and once the hedge-pig whined.  Third Witch  Harpier cries 'Tis time, 'tis time.  First Witch  Round about the cauldron go; In the poison'd entrails throw. Toad, that under cold stone Days and nights has thirty-one Swelter'd venom sleeping got, Boil thou first i' the charmed pot.
