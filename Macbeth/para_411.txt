LADY MACBETH :  The thane of Fife had a wife: where is she now?-- What, will these hands ne'er be clean?--No more o' that, my lord, no more o' that: you mar all with this starting.  Doctor  Go to, go to; you have known what you should not.  Gentlewoman  She has spoke what she should not, I am sure of that: heaven knows what she has known.
