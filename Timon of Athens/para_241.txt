HORTENSIUS :  I'm weary of this charge, the gods can witness: I know my lord hath spent of Timon's wealth, And now ingratitude makes it worse than stealth. Varro's  First Servant  Yes, mine's three thousand crowns: what's yours? Lucilius' Servant	Five thousand mine. Varro's  First Servant  'Tis much deep: and it should seem by the sun, Your master's confidence was above mine; Else, surely, his had equall'd. Enter FLAMINIUS.
