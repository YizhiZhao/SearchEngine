TIMON :  Be't not in thy care; go, I charge thee, invite them all: let in the tide Of knaves once more; my cook and I'll provide. Exeunt  SCENE V. The same. The senate-house. The Senate sitting. First Senator  My lord, you have my voice to it; the fault's Bloody; 'tis necessary he should die: Nothing emboldens sin so much as mercy.  Second Senator  Most true; the law shall bruise him.
