DUKE OF AUMERLE :  You holy clergymen, is there no plot To rid the realm of this pernicious blot?  Abbot  My lord, Before I freely speak my mind herein, You shall not only take the sacrament To bury mine intents, but also to effect Whatever I shall happen to devise. I see your brows are full of discontent, Your hearts of sorrow and your eyes of tears: Come home with me to supper; and I'll lay A plot shall show us all a merry day. Exeunt
