HENRY BOLINGBROKE :  Then, England's ground, farewell; sweet soil, adieu; My mother, and my nurse, that bears me yet! Where'er I wander, boast of this I can, Though banish'd, yet a trueborn Englishman. Exeunt  SCENE IV. The court.
