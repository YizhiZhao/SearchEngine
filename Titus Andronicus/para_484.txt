TITUS ANDRONICUS :  Fie, Publius, fie! thou art too much deceived; The one is Murder, Rape is the other's name; And therefore bind them, gentle Publius. Caius and Valentine, lay hands on them. Oft have you heard me wish for such an hour, And now I find it; therefore bind them sure, And stop their mouths, if they begin to cry. Exit PUBLIUS,  &amp; c. lay hold on CHIRON and DEMETRIUS
