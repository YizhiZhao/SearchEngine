KING HENRY VIII :  There's his period, To sheathe his knife in us. He is attach'd; Call him to present trial: if he may Find mercy in the law, 'tis his: if none, Let him not seek 't of us: by day and night, He's traitor to the height. Exeunt  SCENE III. An ante-chamber in the palace.  Enter Chamberlain and SANDS  Chamberlain  Is't possible the spells of France should juggle Men into such strange mysteries?
