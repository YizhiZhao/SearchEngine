KING HENRY VIII :  Give her an hundred marks. I'll to the queen. Exit  Old Lady  An hundred marks! By this light, I'll ha' more. An ordinary groom is for such payment. I will have more, or scold it out of him. Said I for this, the girl was like to him? I will have more, or else unsay't; and now, While it is hot, I'll put it to the issue. Exeunt  SCENE II.
