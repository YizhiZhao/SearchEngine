KING HENRY VIII :  Now, by thy looks I guess thy message. Is the queen deliver'd? Say, ay; and of a boy.  Old Lady  Ay, ay, my liege; And of a lovely boy: the God of heaven Both now and ever bless her! 'tis a girl, Promises boys hereafter. Sir, your queen Desires your visitation, and to be Acquainted with this stranger 'tis as like you As cherry is to cherry.
