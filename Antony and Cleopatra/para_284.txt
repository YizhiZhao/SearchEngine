MARK ANTONY :  Say to me, Whose fortunes shall rise higher, Caesar's or mine?  Soothsayer  Caesar's. Therefore, O Antony, stay not by his side: Thy demon, that's thy spirit which keeps thee, is Noble, courageous high, unmatchable, Where Caesar's is not; but, near him, thy angel Becomes a fear, as being o'erpower'd: therefore Make space enough between you.
