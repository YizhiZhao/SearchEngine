WARWICK :  This is his tent; and see where stand his guard. Courage, my masters! honour now or never! But follow me, and Edward shall be ours.  First Watchman  Who goes there?  Second Watchman  Stay, or thou diest! WARWICK and the rest cry all, 'Warwick! Warwick!'  and set upon the Guard, who fly, crying, 'Arm! arm!' WARWICK and the rest following them The drum playing and trumpet sounding, reenter  WARWICK, SOMERSET, and the rest, bringing KING  EDWARD IV out in his gown, sitting in a chair. RICHARD and HASTINGS fly over the stage
