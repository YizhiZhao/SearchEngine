JESSICA :  Farewell, good Launcelot. Exit Launcelot Alack, what heinous sin is it in me To be ashamed to be my father's child! But though I am a daughter to his blood, I am not to his manners. O Lorenzo, If thou keep promise, I shall end this strife, Become a Christian and thy loving wife. Exit  SCENE IV. The same. A street.  Enter GRATIANO, LORENZO, SALARINO, and SALANIO
