GLOUCESTER :  If I thou protector of this damned strumpet-- Tellest thou me of 'ifs'?  Thou art a traitor: Off with his head! Now, by Saint Paul I swear, I will not dine until I see the same. Lovel and Ratcliff, look that it be done: The rest, that love me, rise and follow me. Exeunt all but HASTINGS, RATCLIFF, and LOVEL
