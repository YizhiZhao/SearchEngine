PERICLES :  I'll show the virtue I have borne in arms.  First Fisherman  Why, do 'e take it, and the gods give thee good on't!  Second Fisherman  Ay, but hark you, my friend; 'twas we that made up this garment through the rough seams of the waters: there are certain condolements, certain vails. I hope, sir, if you thrive, you'll remember from whence you had it.
