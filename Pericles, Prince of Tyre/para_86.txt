PERICLES :  He is a happy king, since he gains from his subjects the name of good by his government. How far is his court distant from this shore?  First Fisherman  Marry, sir, half a day's journey: and I'll tell you, he hath a fair daughter, and to-morrow is her birth-day; and there are princes and knights come from all parts of the world to just and tourney for her love.
