PERICLES :  Believe 't, I will. By your furtherance I am clothed in steel; And, spite of all the rapture of the sea, This jewel holds his building on my arm: Unto thy value I will mount myself Upon a courser, whose delightful steps Shall make the gazer joy to see him tread. Only, my friend, I yet am unprovided Of a pair of bases.  Second Fisherman  We'll sure provide: thou shalt have my best gown to make thee a pair; and I'll bring thee to the court myself.
