PERICLES :  Were my fortunes equal to my desires, I could wish to make one there.  First Fisherman  O, sir, things must be as they may; and what a man cannot get, he may lawfully deal for--his wife's soul. Re-enter Second and Third Fishermen, drawing up a net  Second Fisherman  Help, master, help! here's a fish hangs in the net, like a poor man's right in the law; 'twill hardly come out. Ha! bots on't, 'tis come at last, and 'tis turned to a rusty armour.
