PERICLES :  [Aside]  A pretty moral.  Third Fisherman  But, master, if I had been the sexton, I would have been that day in the belfry.  Second Fisherman  Why, man?  Third Fisherman  Because he should have swallowed me too: and when I had been in his belly, I would have kept such a jangling of the bells, that he should never have left, till he cast bells, steeple, church, and parish up again.
