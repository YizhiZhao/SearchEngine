ACT IV : SCENE I. The frontiers of Mantua. A forest.  Enter certain Outlaws  First Outlaw  Fellows, stand fast; I see a passenger.  Second Outlaw  If there be ten, shrink not, but down with 'em. Enter VALENTINE and SPEED  Third Outlaw  Stand, sir, and throw us that you have about ye: If not: we'll make you sit and rifle you.
