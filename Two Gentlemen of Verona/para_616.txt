VALENTINE :  I take your offer and will live with you, Provided that you do no outrages On silly women or poor passengers.  Third Outlaw  No, we detest such vile base practises. Come, go with us, we'll bring thee to our crews, And show thee all the treasure we have got, Which, with ourselves, all rest at thy dispose. Exeunt  SCENE II. Milan.
