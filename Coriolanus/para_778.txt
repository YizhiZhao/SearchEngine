AUFIDIUS :  I have not deserved it. But, worthy lords, have you with heed perused What I have written to you?  Lords  We have.  First Lord  And grieve to hear't. What faults he made before the last, I think Might have found easy fines: but there to end Where he was to begin and give away The benefit of our levies, answering us With our own charge, making a treaty where There was a yielding,--this admits no excuse.
