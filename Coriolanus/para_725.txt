MENENIUS :  I neither care for the world nor your general: for such things as you, I can scarce think there's any, ye're so slight. He that hath a will to die by himself fears it not from another: let your general do his worst. For you, be that you are, long; and your misery increase with your age! I say to you, as I was said to, Away! Exit  First Senator  A noble fellow, I warrant him.  Second Senator  The worthy fellow is our general: he's the rock, the oak not to be wind-shaken.
