MENENIUS :  Consider this: he has been bred i' the wars Since he could draw a sword, and is ill school'd In bolted language; meal and bran together He throws without distinction. Give me leave, I'll go to him, and undertake to bring him Where he shall answer, by a lawful form, In peace, to his utmost peril.  First Senator  Noble tribunes, It is the humane way: the other course Will prove too bloody, and the end of it Unknown to the beginning.
