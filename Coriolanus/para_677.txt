SICINIUS :  Go, masters, get you home; be not dismay'd: These are a side that would be glad to have This true which they so seem to fear. Go home, And show no sign of fear.  First Citizen  The gods be good to us! Come, masters, let's home. I ever said we were i' the wrong when we banished him.  Second Citizen  So did we all. But, come, let's home. Exeunt Citizens
