COMINIUS :  I tell you, he does sit in gold, his eye Red as 'twould burn Rome; and his injury The gaoler to his pity. I kneel'd before him; 'Twas very faintly he said 'Rise;' dismiss'd me Thus, with his speechless hand: what he would do, He sent in writing after me; what he would not, Bound with an oath to yield to his conditions: So that all hope is vain. Unless his noble mother, and his wife; Who, as I hear, mean to solicit him For mercy to his country. Therefore, let's hence, And with our fair entreaties haste them on. Exeunt  SCENE II. Entrance of the Volscian camp before Rome.  Two Sentinels on guard. Enter to them, MENENIUS  First Senator  Stay: whence are you?  Second Senator  Stand, and go back.
