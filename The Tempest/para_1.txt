ACT I : SCENE I. On a ship at sea: a tempestuous noise  of thunder and lightning heard. Enter a Master and a Boatswain  Master  Boatswain!  Boatswain  Here, master: what cheer?  Master  Good, speak to the mariners: fall to't, yarely, or we run ourselves aground: bestir, bestir. Exit Enter Mariners  Boatswain  Heigh, my hearts! cheerly, cheerly, my hearts! yare, yare! Take in the topsail. Tend to the master's whistle. Blow, till thou burst thy wind, if room enough! Enter ALONSO, SEBASTIAN, ANTONIO, FERDINAND, GONZALO, and others
