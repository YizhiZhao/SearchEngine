'ARTEMIDORUS.' : Here will I stand till Caesar pass along, And as a suitor will I give him this. My heart laments that virtue cannot live Out of the teeth of emulation. If thou read this, O Caesar, thou mayst live; If not, the Fates with traitors do contrive. Exit  SCENE IV. Another part of the same street, before the house of BRUTUS.  Enter PORTIA and LUCIUS
