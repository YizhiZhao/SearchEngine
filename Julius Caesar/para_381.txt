CINNA THE POET :  I am not Cinna the conspirator.  Fourth Citizen  It is no matter, his name's Cinna; pluck but his name out of his heart, and turn him going.  Third Citizen  Tear him, tear him! Come, brands ho! fire-brands: to Brutus', to Cassius'; burn all: some to Decius' house, and some to Casca's; some to Ligarius': away, go! Exeunt
