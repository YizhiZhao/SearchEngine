CINNA THE POET :  I dreamt to-night that I did feast with Caesar, And things unlucky charge my fantasy: I have no will to wander forth of doors, Yet something leads me forth. Enter Citizens  First Citizen  What is your name?  Second Citizen  Whither are you going?  Third Citizen  Where do you dwell?  Fourth Citizen  Are you a married man or a bachelor?  Second Citizen  Answer every man directly.  First Citizen  Ay, and briefly.  Fourth Citizen  Ay, and wisely.  Third Citizen  Ay, and truly, you were best.
