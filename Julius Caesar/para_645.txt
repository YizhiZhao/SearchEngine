LUCILIUS :  Only I yield to die: There is so much that thou wilt kill me straight; Offering money Kill Brutus, and be honour'd in his death.  First Soldier  We must not. A noble prisoner!  Second Soldier  Room, ho! Tell Antony, Brutus is ta'en.  First Soldier  I'll tell the news. Here comes the general. Enter ANTONY Brutus is ta'en, Brutus is ta'en, my lord.
