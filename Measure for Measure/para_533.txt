DUKE VINCENTIO :  Nor, gentle daughter, fear you not at all. He is your husband on a pre-contract: To bring you thus together, 'tis no sin, Sith that the justice of your title to him Doth flourish the deceit. Come, let us go: Our corn's to reap, for yet our tithe's to sow. Exeunt  SCENE II. A room in the prison.  Enter Provost and POMPEY  Provost  Come hither, sirrah. Can you cut off a man's head?
