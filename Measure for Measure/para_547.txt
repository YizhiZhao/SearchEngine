CLAUDIO :  As fast lock'd up in sleep as guiltless labour When it lies starkly in the traveller's bones: He will not wake.  Provost  Who can do good on him? Well, go, prepare yourself. Knocking within But, hark, what noise? Heaven give your spirits comfort! Exit CLAUDIO By and by. I hope it is some pardon or reprieve For the most gentle Claudio. Enter DUKE VINCENTIO disguised as before Welcome father.
