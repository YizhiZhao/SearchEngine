DUKE VINCENTIO :  A creature unprepared, unmeet for death; And to transport him in the mind he is Were damnable.  Provost  Here in the prison, father, There died this morning of a cruel fever One Ragozine, a most notorious pirate, A man of Claudio's years; his beard and head Just of his colour. What if we do omit This reprobate till he were well inclined; And satisfy the deputy with the visage Of Ragozine, more like to Claudio?
