COUNTESS :  Why should he be killed?  Clown  So say I, madam, if he run away, as I hear he does: the danger is in standing to't; that's the loss of men, though it be the getting of children. Here they come will tell you more: for my part, I only hear your son was run away. Exit Enter HELENA, and two Gentlemen  First Gentleman  Save you, good madam.
