PISTOL :  Base tike, call'st thou me host? Now, by this hand, I swear, I scorn the term; Nor shall my Nell keep lodgers.  Hostess  No, by my troth, not long; for we cannot lodge and board a dozen or fourteen gentlewomen that live honestly by the prick of their needles, but it will be thought we keep a bawdy house straight. NYM and PISTOL draw O well a day, Lady, if he be not drawn now! we shall see wilful adultery and murder committed.
