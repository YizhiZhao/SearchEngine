DAUPHIN :  Shall we go send them dinners and fresh suits And give their fasting horses provender, And after fight with them?  Constable  I stay but for my guidon: to the field! I will the banner from a trumpet take, And use it for my haste. Come, come, away! The sun is high, and we outwear the day. Exeunt  SCENE III. The English camp.
