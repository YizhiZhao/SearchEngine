FLUELLEN :  I tell you what, Captain Gower; I do perceive he is not the man that he would gladly make show to the world he is: if I find a hole in his coat, I will tell him my mind. Drum heard Hark you, the king is coming, and I must speak with him from the pridge. Drum and colours. Enter KING HENRY, GLOUCESTER, and Soldiers God pless your majesty!
