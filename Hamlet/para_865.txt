HAMLET :  How absolute the knave is! we must speak by the card, or equivocation will undo us. By the Lord, Horatio, these three years I have taken a note of it; the age is grown so picked that the toe of the peasant comes so near the heel of the courtier, he gaffs his kibe. How long hast thou been a grave-maker?  First Clown  Of all the days i' the year, I came to't that day that our last king Hamlet overcame Fortinbras.
