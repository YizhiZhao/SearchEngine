KING CLAUDIUS :  Break not your sleeps for that: you must not think That we are made of stuff so flat and dull That we can let our beard be shook with danger And think it pastime. You shortly shall hear more: I loved your father, and we love ourself; And that, I hope, will teach you to imagine-- Enter a Messenger How now! what news?  Messenger  Letters, my lord, from Hamlet: This to your majesty; this to the queen.
