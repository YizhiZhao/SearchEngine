TYBALT :  What, drawn, and talk of peace! I hate the word, As I hate hell, all Montagues, and thee: Have at thee, coward! They fight Enter, several of both houses, who join the fray; then enter Citizens, with clubs  First Citizen  Clubs, bills, and partisans! strike! beat them down!
