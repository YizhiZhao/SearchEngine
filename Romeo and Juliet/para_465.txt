FRIAR LAURENCE :  Hark, how they knock! Who's there? Romeo, arise; Thou wilt be taken. Stay awhile! Stand up; Knocking Run to my study. By and by! God's will, What simpleness is this! I come, I come! Knocking Who knocks so hard? whence come you? what's your will?  Nurse  [Within]  Let me come in, and you shall know my errand; I come from Lady Juliet.
