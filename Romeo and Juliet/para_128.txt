LADY CAPULET :  Well, think of marriage now; younger than you, Here in Verona, ladies of esteem, Are made already mothers: by my count, I was your mother much upon these years That you are now a maid. Thus then in brief: The valiant Paris seeks you for his love.  Nurse  A man, young lady! lady, such a man As all the world--why, he's a man of wax.
