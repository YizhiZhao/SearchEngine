ROMEO :  A gentleman, nurse, that loves to hear himself talk, and will speak more in a minute than he will stand to in a month.  Nurse  An a' speak any thing against me, I'll take him down, an a' were lustier than he is, and twenty such Jacks; and if I cannot, I'll find those that shall. Scurvy knave! I am none of his flirt-gills; I am none of his skains-mates. And thou must stand by too, and suffer every knave to use me at his pleasure?
