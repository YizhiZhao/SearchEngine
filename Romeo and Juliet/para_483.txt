CAPULET :  Well get you gone: o' Thursday be it, then. Go you to Juliet ere you go to bed, Prepare her, wife, against this wedding-day. Farewell, my lord. Light to my chamber, ho! Afore me! it is so very very late, That we may call it early by and by. Good night. Exeunt  SCENE V. Capulet's orchard.  Enter ROMEO and JULIET above, at the window
