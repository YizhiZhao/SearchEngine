PETER :  O, I cry you mercy; you are the singer: I will say for you. It is 'music with her silver sound,' because musicians have no gold for sounding: 'Then music with her silver sound With speedy help doth lend redress.' Exit  First Musician  What a pestilent knave is this same!  Second Musician  Hang him, Jack! Come, we'll in here; tarry for the mourners, and stay dinner. Exeunt
