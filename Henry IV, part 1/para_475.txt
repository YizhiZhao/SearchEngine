FALSTAFF :  God-a-mercy! so should I be sure to be heart-burned. Enter Hostess How now, Dame Partlet the hen! have you inquired yet who picked my pocket?  Hostess  Why, Sir John, what do you think, Sir John? do you think I keep thieves in my house? I have searched, I have inquired, so has my husband, man by man, boy by boy, servant by servant: the tithe of a hair was never lost in my house before.
