PRINCE HENRY :  The man, I do assure you, is not here; For I myself at this time have employ'd him. And, sheriff, I will engage my word to thee That I will, by to-morrow dinner-time, Send him to answer thee, or any man, For any thing he shall be charged withal: And so let me entreat you leave the house.  Sheriff  I will, my lord. There are two gentlemen Have in this robbery lost three hundred marks.
