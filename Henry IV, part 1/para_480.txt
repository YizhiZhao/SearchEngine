FALSTAFF :  How! poor? look upon his face; what call you rich? let them coin his nose, let them coin his cheeks: Ill not pay a denier. What, will you make a younker of me? shall I not take mine case in mine inn but I shall have my pocket picked? I have lost a seal-ring of my grandfather's worth forty mark.  Hostess  O Jesu, I have heard the prince tell him, I know not how oft, that ring was copper!
